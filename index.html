01 / About

The Bridge Between Tech & Business

I'm a customer-facing technical professional with over 10 years of experience across solutions engineering, pre-sales, distributed systems architecture, and applied AI. I've spent my career at the intersection of engineering depth and business impact — translating complex platforms into measurable outcomes for enterprise customers.

My work at ScyllaDB took me through Fortune 500 enterprise cycles — leading PoCs, architecture reviews, and technical workshops for workloads at tens of millions of users. Before that, at SQream, I delivered production rollouts for global leaders like SK Telecom, SK Hynix, and LG Uplus.

Today I'm building at the frontier: HeyBirdie.AI is my applied AI project — a voice agent platform integrating real-time LLM workflows, streaming inference, and multi-turn conversation design. Alongside it, Datanised continues to serve enterprise clients with cloud-native architecture and data platform consulting.

I'm at my best when I'm running a discovery workshop, scoping a PoC, or designing a system that genuinely solves a hard problem. I bring engineering credibility, commercial awareness, and a deep drive to make technology actually work for the people using it.
